Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is peanut butter and jelly with a twist. The kids will thank you!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Heat a griddle to 350 degrees F
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on unbuttered side of one slice of bread
  4. Spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side
  5. Place bread slices together so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle
